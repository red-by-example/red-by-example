.dcs
USAGE:
     AS-RGBA a b c d

DESCRIPTION: 
     Combine a, b, c and d values into a tuple. 
     AS-RGBA is a routine! value.

ARGUMENTS:
     a            [integer!] 
     b            [integer!] 
     c            [integer!] 
     d            [integer!] 

.dce
.box as-rgba
>> as-rgba 255 0 255 1
== 255.0.255.1
