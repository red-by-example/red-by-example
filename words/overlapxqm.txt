.dcs
USAGE:
     OVERLAP? A B

DESCRIPTION: 
     Return TRUE if the two faces bounding boxes are overlapping. 
     OVERLAP? is a function! value.

ARGUMENTS:
     A            [object!] "First face."
     B            [object!] "Second face."

RETURNS:
     TRUE if overlapping.
     [logic!]
