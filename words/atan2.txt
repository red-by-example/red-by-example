The word $$atan2$$ ($$arctangent$$) has 2 arguments x and y.
It returns the angle of the point y/x in radians, when measured counterclockwise
from a circle's x axis (where 0, 0  represents the center of the circle).

The return value is between -$$pi$$ and +$$pi$$.
.pre
red>> atan2 1 1
== 0.7853981633974483
%%45 degrees%%
