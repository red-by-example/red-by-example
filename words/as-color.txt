The word $$as-color$$ generates a $$tuple!$$ with R, G and B values
from its 3 $$integer!$$ arguments.
.pre
red>> as-color 255 0 0
== 255.0.0

.box Predefined colors (e.g. $$0red$$ $$0green$$ etc.) are available also
red>> red
== 255.0.0

red>> green
== 0.255.0

red>> yellow
== 255.255.0
