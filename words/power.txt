The word $$power$$ returns the value of a $$number!$$ raised to a given power (exponent).

It is the functional version of the infix word $$**$$.

It has 2 arguments: a base value and a power, both $$number!$$s.
.pre
red>> power 3.1 2
== 9.61

red>> power 100.9 0.5
== 10.04489920307815

red>> power 2 10
== 1024
