The word $$lesser?$$ returns $$true$$ if the first argumjent is 
less than the second argument, otherwise $$false$$.  

It is the functional equivalent of the infix word $$<$$.
.pre
red>> lesser? 33 44
== true

red>> lesser? [8 5 2] [ 7 5 2]
== false

red>> lesser? [6 5 2] [ 7 5 2]
== true
