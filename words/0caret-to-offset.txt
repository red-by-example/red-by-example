USAGE:
     CARET-TO-OFFSET face pos

DESCRIPTION: 
     Given a text position, returns the corresponding coordinate relative
     to the top-left of the layout box. 
     CARET-TO-OFFSET is a function! value.

ARGUMENTS:
     face         [object!] 
     pos          [integer!] 

REFINEMENTS:
     /lower       => lower end offset of the caret.

RETURNS:
     [pair!]
