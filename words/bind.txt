.dcs
USAGE:
     BIND word context

DESCRIPTION: 
     Bind words to a context; returns rebound words. 
     BIND is a native! value.

ARGUMENTS:
     word         [block! any-word!] 
     context      [any-word! any-object! function!] 

REFINEMENTS:
     /copy        => Deep copy blocks before binding.

RETURNS:
     [block! any-word!]
