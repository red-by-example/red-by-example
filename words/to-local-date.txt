.dcs
USAGE:
     TO-LOCAL-DATE date

DESCRIPTION: 
     Returns the date with local zone. 
     TO-LOCAL-DATE is a function! value.

ARGUMENTS:
     date         [date!] 

RETURNS:
     [date!]
.dce

See $$date!$$
.box to-local-date - note added zone time-difference
>> to-local-date 3-June-2020
== 3-Jun-2020/1:00:00+01:00
