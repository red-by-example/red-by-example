The infix word $$**$$ raises argument 1 to the power argument 2.
.pre
red>> 2 ** 2
== 4

red>> 2 ** 10
== 1024

red>> 2 ** -1
== 0.5

red>> 2 ** -10
== 0.0009765625

red>> -2 ** 4
== 16

red>> -2 ** 3
== -8

red>> 2 ** 0.5
== 1.414213562373095

red>> 2 ** -0.5
== 0.7071067811865476
